Title: Grover's Algorithm

Grover's algorithm was developed by Lov Grover in 1996.
It is a quantum algorithm used for searching unsorted databases.

The algorithm provides a quadratic speedup compared to classical search algorithms.
While classical search requires O(n) time, Grover's algorithm works in O(sqrt(n)) time.

Grover's algorithm is useful in optimization and search problems.
It is one of the key examples of quantum advantage.
