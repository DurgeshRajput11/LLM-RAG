Title: Timeline of Quantum Computing

In 1982, Richard Feynman proposed the idea of quantum computing.
In 1994, Peter Shor introduced Shor's algorithm for factoring integers.

In 1996, Lov Grover developed Grover's algorithm for database search.

These milestones marked the early development of quantum computing.

Over time, research expanded into building quantum hardware and improving algorithms.
Quantum computing continues to evolve as a promising field in computer science.
